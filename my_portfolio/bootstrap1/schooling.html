MISSION

Mission of Dharam Samaj Bal Mandir Sr. Sec. School is to create such a community as is ever ready to meet challenges.
We will inspire every child to reach his full potential. We will value every child as an individual in his thorough development, encouraging educational excellence, to enable them to leave us as model citizens of the future.

InfraStructure

Library

Our school Dharam Samaj Bal Mandir Sr. Sec. School has also well-equipped Library dedicated to students of different age groups. Its extensive collections of books, journals, electronic media, including magazines in English, and Hindi will focus on providing an excellent reference environment.

Chemistry Lab

We are here greeted by the pungent smell of Ammonia, which is a testimony of a very active Chemistry Lab. Under the strict and alert vigilance of teachers and assistant, students perform various tests and analysis of salt and solution

Computer lab

The campus has separate computer labs for sr. students as well as jr. Students. The lab caters to the students learning C++ in classes XI and XII and students of class IX learning Oracle database programming, HTML and DHTML for classes VI to VIII, basics for primary classes.
